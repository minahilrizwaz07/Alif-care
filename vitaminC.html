Vitamin C is a powerful antioxidant that helps brighten your skin, reduce dark spots, and boost collagen production. Using a Vitamin C serum regularly can improve overall skin tone and texture.

Vitamin C Benefits

  • Brightens dull skin and enhances glow
  • Reduces dark spots and hyperpigmentation
  • Protects against sun damage and free radicals
  • Boosts collagen production for firmer skin

Vitamin C Rich Foods

Incorporate foods like oranges, lemons, strawberries, kiwis, and bell peppers into your diet for naturally glowing skin.

How to Use Vitamin C Serum

  • Apply 3–5 drops on clean skin in the morning
  • Follow with moisturizer and sunscreen
  • Can be combined with Niacinamide for enhanced brightening
