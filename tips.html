Niacinamide + Vitamin C = Glowing Skin

Niacinamide and Vitamin C work synergistically to brighten your skin tone and reduce dark spots. Niacinamide also improves skin texture and strengthens the skin barrier, leaving your skin smooth and healthy.

Ideal for combination and sensitive skin types, this combination helps maintain a clear and radiant complexion.

Glycolic Acid vs Salicylic Acid

Glycolic acid exfoliates dead skin cells, improves texture, and brightens dull skin. Salicylic acid penetrates pores to remove excess oil and prevent acne breakouts. Choose the acid based on your skin type and concern.

Eat Vitamin C Rich Foods

Include oranges, lemons, strawberries, kiwis, and bell peppers in your diet to naturally brighten your skin. Vitamin C boosts collagen production, protects against sun damage, and enhances overall skin health.

Daily Skincare Routine for Healthy Skin

Follow a simple 3-step routine: cleanse, moisturize, and protect. Sunscreen is essential during the day to prevent premature aging and dark spots.

  • Morning: Cleanser → Vitamin C Serum → Moisturizer → Sunscreen
  • Night: Cleanser → Niacinamide or Retinol → Moisturizer

Preventing Acne & Breakouts

Keep your skin clean, avoid heavy makeup, and use non-comedogenic products. Salicylic acid cleansers and tea tree oil products are effective for acne-prone skin.
